About

This is the website of the Hashkafa Circle, a kollel headed by Rabbi Triebitz. On this site you can watch and hear shiurim from Rabbi Triebitz, and download and read Reshimu, the journal of the Hashkafa Circle.

The site is maintained by David Sedley. You can contact me via e-mail: admin @ hashkafacircle.com

Rabbi Triebitz can be contacted via e-mail: rabbitriebitz @ hashkafacircle.com

We welcome your feedback and thoughts about the site, and the material.
